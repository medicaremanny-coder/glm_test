## 👤 Profile

> [!info]+ Who is Brian Tracy?
> **Brian Tracy** (born January 5, 1944, Charlottetown, Canada) is a Canadian-American author, speaker, and Chairman of **Brian Tracy International** (San Diego). He has written **80+ books** translated into dozens of languages and addressed millions of people in talks and seminars across 80+ countries.
>
> His story is pure Rohn philosophy in action: a **high-school dropout** who worked as a laborer and on a Norwegian freighter, then got into sales, asked the top salesman what he was doing differently — and systematized everything he learned. Where [[Jim Rohn]] was the *philosopher*, Tracy is the *engineer* of personal achievement.

## 🧠 Core Ideas — The Tracy System

> [!abstract]- 1. Eat That Frog (priority management)
> Your "frog" is your biggest, most important, most-resisted task. Do it **first thing**, before email, before meetings. If you have two frogs, eat the ugliest one first. Procrastination is the thief of achievement.

> [!abstract]- 2. Goals — in writing, or they don't count
> Only ~3% of adults have clear, **written** goals. Tracy's 7-step loop: decide exactly what you want → write it down → set a deadline → list everything needed → organize the list into a plan → act immediately → **do something every day** toward the goal.

> [!abstract]- 3. The Psychology of Achievement
> Self-concept drives performance: you always act consistently with your inner picture of yourself. Change the self-concept (affirmation, visualization, incremental wins) and the outer results follow.

> [!abstract]- 4. The Law of Cause and Effect
> Success is not luck — it's specific causes producing specific effects. Find people who have the result you want, do what they did, and you get what they got.

> [!abstract]- 5. Continuous learning (the Rohn overlap)
> Read 30–60 minutes daily in your field; turn your car into a "university on wheels" with audio programs. One hour a day of study puts you in the top of your field within a few years.
